ABOUT CETAA

A Legacy That Continues To Inspire

The College of Engineering Trivandrum Alumni Association (CETAA) is a vibrant global community connecting thousands of graduates through mentorship, networking, innovation, infrastructure development and lifelong friendships.

By strengthening alumni engagement and supporting the institution's growth, CETAA continues to build opportunities for current students while preserving the rich heritage of one of India's premier engineering institutions.
